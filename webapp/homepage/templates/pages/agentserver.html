Agent building has never been easier

What is Operatus?

Thanks to the many AI libraries and frameworks available to us today, building AI agents has improved by leaps and bounds. However, deploying agents in production or for more than personal use can be quite brittle, and cryptic, leading to a great deal of frustration.

Operatus makes it easy to rapidly serve agents in both self-hosted and cloud environments. Operatus provides a central platform to monitor and control your entire fleet of agents. Swap out components or add new integrations in production with minimal downtime.

Use well-documented, straightforward CLI tools to tweak and customize your agents. Don't have programming experience? Operatus provides a low-/no-code experience.
