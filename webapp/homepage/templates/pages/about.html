What's in a name?

  • In computer science, an operator is something that interacts with an input that precedes and/or follows it, like the plus sign + will add the two numbers it's between. An abstract object (similar to an interface) is a partial definition that is meant to be completed at a later time rather than used directly. Thus, an abstract operator is a placeholder actor that will be given meaning at a later time based on context.
  • In business, an operator is a person that runs a business, usually in a financial or operational capacity.
  • Tool use or function calling is an important feature of AI agents. We build infrastructure for agents that are operators of tools like search, browsers, and other agents.
  • The humans of abstract operators are the operators of agents, bringing them to market to serve and delight our enterprise customers.
  • We are wielders (operators) of abstraction to make conceptual frameworks work for us by expressing them through multiagent systems.
